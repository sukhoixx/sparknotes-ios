Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

SparkNotes ("the App") is a news app for kids. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from that provider.
  • Profile information: Screen name and preferred news categories you choose within the app.
  • Activity: Articles you like and comments you post.
  • Authentication tokens: A secure token stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To personalize your news feed based on your category preferences
  • To save your likes and display your comments

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers hosted by Railway (railway.app). Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage and are never shared.

Third-Party Sign-In

The App supports sign-in via Apple and Google. When you use these services, you are also subject to their respective privacy policies. We only receive the name and email you authorize.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only used to operate the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for kids and we take their privacy seriously. We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the service. Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion of their child's data.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
